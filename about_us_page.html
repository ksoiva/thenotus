Learn more about us

  • Who we are?

    Who we are?

    We are a group of enthusiastic students who share the belief that time is precious and must be managed properly and wisely. We spent lots of hours studying and understand how important it is to plan your day and control work time. So we decided to create this platform.

  • Our purpose

    Our purpose

    When we started creating this system, we wanted to create something that didn't require the use of additional resources. Our goal was to develop an application that wasn't cluttered with a bunch of unnecessary features, that would be simple and easy to use. So, we came to the concept of Notus.

  • Our product

    Our product

    Notus helps busy people like you focus on what’s important. It helps to save time and do the things that really matter. We offer you a convenient editor for organizing affairs for the day, as well as a timer to manage your time. We created a system that is enough to solve all user problems in the field of time management.
